About Welbodi na Gentri

Empowering Sierra Leone through health education and wellness

Our Mission

Welbodi na Gentri (Wellness and Strength) is dedicated to providing accessible, reliable, and practical health information to every person in Sierra Leone. We believe that health education is the foundation of a healthier nation.

Our Vision

A Sierra Leone where every person has access to health information, understands how to prevent diseases, and can make informed decisions about their health and wellness. We envision a future where health literacy is universal and preventable diseases are minimized.

Our Core Values

🏥 Accessibility

Health information should be free and available to everyone, regardless of location or circumstances.

✅ Reliability

We provide only accurate, evidence-based information from trusted health sources and professionals.

💙 Compassion

We care deeply about the health outcomes of our community and treat every person with respect and dignity.

🎓 Education

We empower people through knowledge, helping them understand health concepts in simple, practical terms.

🤝 Community

We work together with healthcare providers, organizations, and community members to improve public health.

🌍 Prevention

We focus on disease prevention and health promotion rather than just treatment of illnesses.

What We Offer

  • Health Information: Detailed, easy-to-understand information about diseases, prevention, and treatment
  • Daily Health Tips: Practical daily wellness advice to improve your health and lifestyle
  • Nutrition Guidance: Tips on healthy eating and nutrition for your family
  • Exercise & Wellness: Simple ways to stay active and healthy in daily life
  • Healthcare Directory: Find hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies near you across Sierra Leone
  • Educational Videos: Learn health topics through visual, easy-to-follow videos
  • Local Remedies: Information about traditional and modern health practices
  • Emergency Support: Quick access to emergency services and health hotlines
