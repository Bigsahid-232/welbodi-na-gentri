More Health Tips

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Proper hydration helps maintain body temperature, aids digestion, and supports overall health.

Exercise Daily

Aim for 30 minutes of physical activity daily. Walking, dancing, or any movement you enjoy strengthens your heart and improves mental health.

Get Enough Sleep

Sleep 7-8 hours every night. Quality sleep boosts immunity, improves mood, and helps your body repair and regenerate.

Eat Nutritiously

Include fruits, vegetables, proteins, and whole grains. A balanced diet prevents diseases and keeps you energized throughout the day.

Common Diseases in Sierra Leone

Know the symptoms, prevention, and treatment information

Malaria

A parasitic infection transmitted by mosquitoes

  • Symptoms: Fever, chills, body aches, fatigue
  • Prevention: Use bed nets, insect repellent, antimalarial drugs
  • Treatment: Antimalarial medication prescribed by doctor
Learn More

Typhoid Fever

A bacterial infection spread through contaminated water

  • Symptoms: High fever, headache, weakness, rash
  • Prevention: Clean water, proper sanitation, vaccination
  • Treatment: Antibiotics as prescribed by healthcare provider
Learn More

Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Chronic condition affecting heart and blood vessels

  • Symptoms: Often no symptoms, detected during check-up
  • Prevention: Reduce salt, manage stress, exercise, healthy diet
  • Treatment: Medication and lifestyle changes
