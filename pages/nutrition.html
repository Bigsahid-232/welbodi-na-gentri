Nutrition Guide

Healthy eating with local ingredients and simple daily habits.

Learn how to build balanced meals, support common conditions, and check your BMI with trusted local food choices.

Heart health support

  • Leafy greens, sweet potato, lean fish
  • Tomatoes, okra, groundnuts
  • Low-salt stews and boiled tubers

Stronger energy for everyday life

  • Rice or cassava with beans and vegetables
  • Fresh fruits like banana and pawpaw
  • Protein from fish, eggs, or groundnuts

Healthy digestion

  • Warm ginger tea, cooked greens, and millet porridge
  • Fresh fruits and clean water
  • Light meals after heavy activity

Stronger child growth

  • Milk, eggs, beans, and greens
  • Small, frequent balanced meals
  • Fruits, fresh water, and gentle play
