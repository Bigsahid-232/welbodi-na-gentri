Your Health, Your Wealth

Reliable, local health insights custom-tailored for Sierra Leonean families.

Free Health Care Initiative

Completely free clinical treatment is provided under government programs for pregnant women, breastfeeding mothers, and children under the age of 5 across public clinics.

Malaria Intervention

Malaria is fully preventable. Sleep under treated bed nets nightly, keep surrounding water from pooling, and head directly to a clinic for a checkup if fever starts.

Clean Hygiene & Sanitation

Prevent stomach sickness and cholera. Use running water and hand soap consistently, and purify drinking water with local chlorine treatments or boiling methods.
