About the Initiative

Welbodi na Gentri is an independent, non-commercial public health educational portal. Translating literally to “Health is Wealth”, our focus remains on providing clear information.

By offering small, high-contrast, data-efficient content, we help ensure families from urban Freetown districts to rural provincial villages can navigate core medical resources easily.

Important Medical Disclaimer

All details published on this page are intended exclusively for educational overview. They are not direct medical advice. Always consult with certified medical experts at an established health clinic for all diagnoses.
